Rated for 2000+ charge cycles. At one full cycle per day, that's over 5 years of daily use. The battery warranty covers 5 full years from date of purchase.
In UPS mode, iNYX switches to battery power in under 6 milliseconds — imperceptible to your router, computer, TV, or any sensitive equipment. Eco mode switches in under 25ms.
The 1075VA model runs lights, fans, TV, router and small appliances comfortably. For ACs, the 2500VA or 5000VA models are recommended depending on your AC tonnage and other concurrent loads.
Yes. All iNYX models include an MPPT solar charge controller. Connect compatible solar panels to the side terminals. Solar panels are sold separately.
Yes. The iNYX BLE app (Android & iOS) shows battery SOC, backup time, current load, and charging mode. You can also configure system settings from your phone.
Eco mode accepts wide voltage range (110–290V) and passes grid power through when available — saves electricity, slightly longer switchover. UPS mode uses a narrower input range but switches in <6ms, ideal for computers and sensitive equipment.
The iNYX unit mounts on any wall using the included bracket. All connections are on the side panel — solar input, AC mains, and output socket. No separate battery installation needed. Takes approximately 30 minutes.
